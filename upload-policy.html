Policy

Upload And Content Policy

Effective date: May 23, 2026

Purpose Of Uploads

Baseline Share is for useful baseline projects that help people learn, build, and improve their profiles. Good uploads should include understandable source files, clear setup notes, and a project idea that another student or builder can realistically customize.

Allowed Content

Allowed uploads include starter apps, dashboards, analytics projects, portfolio templates, documentation examples, classroom-friendly prototypes, and other project baselines that are safe to download and study.

Not Allowed

Do not upload malware, exploit kits, credential stealers, phishing pages, spam tools, pirated code, private datasets, confidential files, copyrighted material you do not have permission to share, adult content, hateful content, or anything designed to harm users, systems, schools, companies, or communities.

No Secrets Or Private Data

Before uploading, remove API keys, passwords, tokens, private certificates, database dumps, personal documents, private chats, school records, employer files, and any other sensitive information. Users are responsible for the files they upload.

Quality Expectations

A useful baseline should have a clear title, honest description, relevant stack tags, and files that match the description. Misleading uploads, empty archives, broken files, or projects made only to earn credits may be removed.

Review And Removal

Baseline Share may review, hide, or remove uploads at any time. Reports about unsafe, infringing, or misleading content can be sent to lenovo2003@naver.com.

License Responsibility

Uploaders should include license information when possible. If no license is included, users should not assume they have broad permission to reuse the project beyond personal learning or evaluation.
