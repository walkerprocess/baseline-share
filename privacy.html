Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 23, 2026

Information We Collect

Baseline Share may collect the name and email address a user provides when creating a basic account. The site may also store account activity such as uploaded projects, saved favorites, ad-free download credits, and download history. Uploaded project files and related metadata may be stored so that other users can access them.

How We Use Information

Information is used to operate the project library, maintain user sessions, save favorites, track download credits, show download history, and make uploaded baseline projects available to other users. Contact information may be used to respond to support, policy, copyright, privacy, or safety requests.

Cookies And Sessions

Baseline Share uses a session cookie to keep users signed in. Browser storage may also be used for local display preferences such as theme selection. These technologies help the site provide account features and improve the user experience.

Uploaded Content

Uploaded projects may become publicly available for download. Users should not upload private information, secrets, passwords, API keys, confidential school or employer materials, or files they do not have permission to share. Users are responsible for reviewing their files before uploading them.

Advertising

Baseline Share may use advertising or sponsor placements in the future. If third-party ads are enabled, those services may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own privacy policies. The site currently includes a sponsor-view flow as a product prototype, but real paid advertising requires account approval and additional configuration.

Data Sharing

Baseline Share does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the site, comply with legal obligations, protect users, respond to abuse, or work with infrastructure providers such as hosting, storage, analytics, or advertising services.
