About

Helping Builders Start With Better Baselines

Baseline Share exists to help students, early-career builders, and self-taught developers start projects faster. A strong portfolio often begins with a clear baseline: a working structure, thoughtful documentation, and enough direction to let someone build, customize, and explain the work as their own learning journey.

The core idea is simple: share practical baseline project ideas and starter files that people can use to enrich their profiles, resumes, GitHub pages, class portfolios, and internship applications. Instead of staring at a blank folder, users can begin from a useful foundation and spend more time learning, improving, and presenting the project.

Baseline Share is currently operated anonymously while the product is still early. The goal is to keep the focus on the usefulness of the projects rather than on the operator. For contact, questions, or concerns, email lenovo2003@naver.com.

The site is still growing. Over time, Baseline Share aims to support stronger project descriptions, safer uploads, clearer licensing, better moderation, and more project categories for students who want portfolio-ready work with practical learning value.
